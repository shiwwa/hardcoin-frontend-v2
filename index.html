Why it is profitable

All the activities on the platform will be paid for in HardCoin that has a fixed emission. This will also make it easier for customers and fans to pay for certain services..

  • The huge HardCore community will be purchasing HardCoin tokens on a weekly basis to pay for goods, services, tickets, access to private broadcasts, and more.
